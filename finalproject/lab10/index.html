What is it?

Unlike the name suggests, its not a miniature bar where you eat lunch, drink beer, or play darts with friends.

The Micropub web standard is a protocol designed for creating, managing, and deleting content on a personal website or blog using third-party applications. Micropub defines a consistent way for clients to interact with servers to manage content.
